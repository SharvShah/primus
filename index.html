About

Optimus Prime is the courageous leader of the Autobots, the peacekeeping faction of Cybertronians from the planet Cybertron. Originally known as Orion Pax, he rose through ranks and became a symbol of hope against the Decepticon threat.

Leadership & Values

As a leader, Optimus embodies honor, sacrifice, and justice. His decisions are based on moral ground, often putting others before himself. He believes that all life—regardless of origin—deserves freedom.
